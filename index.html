International Backgammon Rules

  • Objective: Move all your checkers into your home board and then bear them off.
  • Movement: Checkers move in opposite directions (Red: clockwise, Green: counterclockwise).
  • Starting position: Standard international setup with 2 checkers on point 24, 5 on point 13, 3 on point 8, and 5 on point 6.
  • Hitting: Landing on a point with a single opponent checker sends it to the bar.
  • Entering from bar: Checkers on the bar must re-enter before any other moves.
  • Bearing off: Exact number required unless all checkers are in the home board and higher numbers can bear off farthest checkers.
  • Doubles: Play all four numbers shown on the dice.
  • Winning: First player to bear off all 15 checkers wins.
